Amazing Cheeseburger Recipe!

Learn how to make the most amazing cheeseburger you will ever taste!

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bun
  • 80/20 fat ground beef
  • sliced tomato
  • Smoked Cheddar
  • Lettuce
  • pickles
  • ketchup and any burger sauce
  • salt, pepper, and italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Season your beef and roll into a patty. Then grill the patty with some olive oil.
  2. Toast your bun with butter
  3. Once the patty is almost ready, add a slice of cheese and melt it.
  4. Then assemble your burger. Kethcup on the bun first, then add your lettuce. Slowly add the patty and lay on the pickles and 2 tomato slices.
  5. Add the top bun and enjoy!
